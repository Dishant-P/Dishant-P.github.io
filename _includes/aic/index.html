Artificial Intelligence Club

Welcome!

I am the founder of the Artifical Intelligence Club for the IEEE GCET SB. My vision for the club is to make a community for the young enthusiasts in the field of Artificial Intelligence and to provide them a platform where they can learn, share and create anything related to this amazing field of Artificial Intelligence. AIC commenced in February 2019. We changed the policy and the workings of AIC in March 2020.

Functioning

We keep different tracks of learning. For each field in AI, we keep a seperate track. The ongoing tracks are: Machine Learning Basics, Python Fundamentals and Data Science. We also keep focused groups. Research Paper Readings is one of them. We conduct weekly meetings to discuss various questions about the topic and to get an update on different tracks.

Since March 2020

Project and Research Guidance

With the new workings of AIC, we added some of the essential needs of a student in their academia. Due to the lack of guidance, students, although enthusiatic and skilled, are not able to showcase or implement their talent into things like projects or research. We provide one-to-one mentoring to students for any of their projects.
